Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

1. No Data Collection

QR Maker Pro operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All QR code generation happens locally on your device.

2. Local Storage Only

We use your browser's localStorage to save your theme preference (light/dark mode) and language selection. This data never leaves your device.

3. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking pixels. Your privacy is completely protected.

4. Third-Party Libraries

We use these open-source libraries loaded from CDNs:

  • QRCode.js - QR code generation
  • JSZip - ZIP file creation
  • FileSaver.js - File downloads

5. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your content.
