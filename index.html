Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to QRGen Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our QR code generator.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: QRGen Pro operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data you input into the application.

Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (theme settings, language selection, color preferences) for a better user experience. This data never leaves your device.

3. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no usage of your information. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party libraries that are loaded from CDN services:

  • QRCode.js - For QR code generation
  • JSZip - For ZIP file creation in bulk generation
  • FileSaver.js - For file download functionality
  • Font Awesome - For icons

These libraries may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies. No analytics are collected.

6. Data Security

Since all data processing happens locally in your browser, your data never leaves your device. This provides the highest level of privacy and security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal data, we are compliant with children's privacy regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
